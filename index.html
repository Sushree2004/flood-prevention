Precautions

  • Stay informed about weather forecasts and flood warnings.
  • Prepare an emergency kit with essential supplies and keep it easily accessible.
  • Keep important documents, such as identification and insurance papers, in a waterproof container.
  • If evacuation is necessary, follow the instructions of local authorities.
  • Avoid walking or driving through floodwaters.
  • Move to higher ground if you notice rising water levels.
